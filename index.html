Passat
B5 & B5.5

The Volkswagen Passat B5 (1996-2000) and B5.5 (2000-2005) are models from the second generation of the Passat series. The B5 features a boxy design with various engine options and comfort features, offering a modern interior and technological amenities. The B5.5 is an updated version of the B5, featuring a more contemporary appearance with redesigned front and rear elements, along with improved technology and comfort inside. Both models are noted for their reliability and spacious interiors.
